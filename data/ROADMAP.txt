COMMON QUESTIONS:

Q: Do I need to follow this exact order?
A: Mostly yes. Supabase setup must be first.
   After that, you need data before you can import.
   UI can be built anytime after data is in Supabase.

Q: Can I test without all customers?
A: Yes. Start with S&S as proof-of-concept.
   Add more customers incrementally.

Q: How do I know if it's working?
A: Follow the checklist. Each step has verification.
   If data appears in Supabase, it's working.

Q: What if I get an error?
A: Check the "STUCK?" section in Implementation_Checklist.txt
   Most errors are environment variables or permissions.

Q: Can Bryan and Joe both work on this?
A: Yes. Joe handles data prep, Bryan handles code.
   Or trade tasks. It's modular.
